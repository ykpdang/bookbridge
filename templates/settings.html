Sync Behavior

Idle gap that splits KOReader page events into separate reading sessions on the stats page.
Use each service's own "position last changed" timestamp to (1) ignore stale re-readings that pretend to be fresh movement and (2) block a client that is materially behind a much newer position from rolling everyone back. Disable only to restore pure percentage-delta leader selection.
A behind client is only vetoed when the peer's position is at least this much newer — keep generous to absorb clock differences between services.
When resetting your progress, check for missing sync data. If found, the system will attempt to regenerate it (which may require re-transcription).
